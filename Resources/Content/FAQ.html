NoMo and You

Who can use NoMo

NoMo is for those who are 18 or over, with internet banking. NoMo works best for those that:

  • Have a monthly income
  • Don't budget their finance
  • Do budget their finance
  • Want a simple way to see how their finances are doing

What is NoMo?

NoMo provides a simple budgeting service that allow individuals to show them how much they're up or down based upon their historic average of funds in their bank account.

How does NoMo work?

NoMo calculates the average of the total sum of your funds from all of your accounts each day of the month using your transactions of the last 3 months. To calculate if your up or down for a particular day, NoMo compares your current sum of funds against your average. For example, if today you have $200 as a total sum of funds and your average is $180, then you are UP $20. This means, you can spend $20 to get back to your average.

How do I report a bug?

Simply send an email to support@nomo.money

Why does NoMo need my details?

NoMo needs your details inorder to connect with your bank account to gain insight for calculations.

Can I add additional bank branches?

Currently only one bank branch can be added.
